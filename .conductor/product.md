# Product Guide: Speckit - Audio Blogging Platform

## Initial Concept
An audio blogging platform with AI-powered therapeutic feedback.

## Primary Users
*   **Audio Blog Creators:** Individuals who wish to record, publish, and share their audio blogs.
*   **Feedback Seekers:** Users interested in receiving AI-powered therapeutic analysis and insights on their recorded audio content.

## Core Features
*   **Audio Management:** Robust functionalities for recording, uploading, storing, and playing back audio content.
*   **AI Therapeutic Feedback:** Integration of AI models to analyze audio blogs and provide therapeutic insights and feedback to users.
    *   **User Management:** Secure user authentication, profile creation, and management capabilities.
+*   **Robust Backend:** Optimized for performance and security, ensuring reliable and safe data handling.
## Non-Functional Requirements & Design Principles
*   **Security & Privacy:** Paramount focus on implementing robust security measures and ensuring privacy for all sensitive user data, including personal information and audio content.
*   **User Experience (UX):** Development of an intuitive, seamless, and user-friendly interface to facilitate easy audio blogging, access to feedback, and overall platform interaction.
