# Product Guidelines: Speckit - Audio Blogging Platform

## Prose Style
**Concise and Direct:** The language used across the platform will be clear, efficient, and to the point. Information will be delivered plainly, without unnecessary embellishment, ensuring users can quickly understand and interact with the application.

## Brand Values
**Empathy and Support:** The core brand value guiding Speckit's visual identity and user interactions will be empathy and support. This means fostering a comforting and understanding environment, particularly given the therapeutic nature of the AI feedback. Design choices and communication will reflect a supportive and user-centric approach.

## Aesthetic Style
**Minimalist and Clean:** The user interface will adhere to a minimalist and clean aesthetic. This approach emphasizes simplicity, functionality, and a clutter-free environment, ensuring easy navigation and a pleasant user experience. Visual elements will be streamlined to focus on core content and functionality.
